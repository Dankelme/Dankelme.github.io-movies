Best Movies Of The Decade According to Daniel

Titanic

Titanic was a great movie in the 90s from the soundtrack, all the way to the smallest detail. Directed by James Cameron, this movie was spectacular.

Saving Private Ryan

Saving Private Ryan was directed by Steven Spielberg, and should be noted as the greatest war movie of all time. the realism in the war scenes is unparallel.

Lord of the Rings

Lord of the Rings was an epic movie, based on the book by J.R.R. Tolkien. This film was not only beautiful to see on the big screen, but defined an era in film making.
